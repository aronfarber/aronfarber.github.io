aesis

from: united states
years active: 2021 - 2021 (as xsphere), 2021 - present (as aesis)
stream/buy: soundcloud, youtube

aesis produced my favorite remix of the entirety of disciple’s “second biggest remix competition of all time” at the age of 13, making him a prodigy in my view. while he did not win, he continues to produce exceptionally boomy dubstep that i really like. his music is filled with ear candy and interesting sound effects, and his flows are unbelievably hard-hitting.

top 3 songs:

  1. mirror temple (2022) with voka - from better together, vol. 3

  2. cuffin season (xsphere remix) (2021) - single

  3. this could be us (xsphere remix) (2021) - single
